DROMA Electronics Phone Service Policy:
Smartphones include a 12-month limited warranty for manufacturing defects.
Physical damage, water damage, and unauthorized repair attempts void warranty coverage.
In-warranty software issues are handled within 2 business days.
Display replacement under paid service is usually completed in 3 to 5 business days.
Proof of purchase is mandatory for all warranty claims.

Extended Phone Coverage Details:
Warranty applies to factory defects in motherboard, power IC, charging IC, speaker receiver, and approved camera modules.
Battery backup reduction due to regular usage is considered wear and may not qualify for free replacement after 6 months.
Display issues caused by pressure cracks, drops, or liquid ingress are excluded from standard warranty.

Phone DOA and Early Failure Policy:
Devices that fail within 48 hours of first activation can be tagged for priority diagnostics.
If defect is confirmed as factory-related within 7 days of purchase, customer may receive replacement approval as per stock.
Replacement requests must include bill copy, IMEI verification, and complete box contents.

Software and Account Support:
System update failures, boot loops after official updates, and factory software corruption are covered in warranty period.
Issues related to forgotten lock credentials, third-party app crashes, or rooted firmware are treated as non-warranty.
Data backup support may be provided as best effort but is not guaranteed before reset or board replacement.

Repair Timelines and Parts:
Charging port, mic, and speaker module replacements are typically completed in 2 to 4 business days.
Camera module and board-level repairs may take 5 to 9 business days.
If imported parts are delayed beyond 10 business days, escalation can be raised through DROMA support.

Warranty Claim Process:
Customer must provide invoice, IMEI number, and registered phone number.
Diagnostic report is shared before paid repair begins for out-of-warranty devices.
Replaced components carry a 90-day service warranty unless otherwise specified.

Warranty Exclusions:
Damage from unauthorized chargers, power surges, accidental drops, fire, theft, or misuse is excluded.
Devices repaired by non-authorized centers are not eligible for manufacturer warranty continuation.
