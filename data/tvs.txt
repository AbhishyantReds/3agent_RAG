DROMA Electronics TV Policy:
Televisions include a 2-year panel warranty and 1-year accessory warranty.
Remote controls and power cables are covered for manufacturing defects for 6 months.
Wall-mount installation support is available at additional charge.
Dead-on-arrival issues reported within 48 hours are eligible for priority replacement review.
On-site inspection is scheduled within 72 hours in serviceable locations.

Extended Television Warranty Scope:
Panel defects such as vertical lines, backlight failure, and dead pixels beyond acceptable threshold are covered within panel warranty terms.
Main board, T-Con board, and power board are covered for manufacturing defects during base warranty period.
Damage due to external impact, voltage spikes without surge protection, or moisture exposure is excluded.

Installation and Demo Policy:
Standard tabletop installation and basic demo are provided in selected cities for eligible models.
Wall-mount bracket cost and custom civil work are not included unless mentioned in sales invoice.
Customer must ensure stable power supply and suitable wall structure before technician visit.

DOA and Replacement Rules:
DOA requests must be raised within 48 hours of delivery and before physical damage signs appear.
Replacement is subject to technical inspection and packaging/accessory completeness.
If replacement stock is unavailable, equivalent model or refund workflow may be offered as per policy.

On-site Service Timelines:
First technician visit target is within 24 to 72 hours in metro and tier-1 locations.
Spare-dependent repairs may take 5 to 12 business days based on logistics.
Repeated unresolved faults within warranty can be escalated for senior engineer assessment.

Accessory and Remote Support:
Remote, stand, and cables have limited accessory coverage; physical breakage is non-warranty.
Paid accessory replacements can be ordered through authorized DROMA channels.

Usage and Care Notes:
Continuous high-brightness operation in non-ventilated rooms can shorten panel life.
Customers are advised to use voltage stabilizer or surge protector in high fluctuation areas.
