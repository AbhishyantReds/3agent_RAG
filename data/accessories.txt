DROMA Electronics Accessories Policy:
Chargers, earphones, and adapters include a 6-month replacement warranty.
Damage due to voltage fluctuation or misuse is excluded from warranty.
Accessories purchased separately require the original bill for support.
Warranty replacement is subject to stock availability.
If an identical accessory is unavailable, an equivalent model may be provided.

Extended Accessory Coverage:
Cables with internal breakage from bending stress and connectors damaged by physical force are treated as non-warranty.
Audio accessories with manufacturing defects such as one-side no sound may be covered within warranty period.
Consumable wear, color fading, and cosmetic degradation are excluded from replacement warranty.

Verification and Claim Steps:
Customer must share purchase invoice, product model, and clear defect photos or video where requested.
Accessories sold as part of combo packs follow bundle-specific warranty conditions.
Serial or batch number verification may be required for adapter and fast-charging components.

Replacement and Turnaround:
In-stock accessory replacements are typically completed in 2 to 4 business days.
If item is unavailable, customer may be offered equivalent compatible accessory after approval.
For discontinued items, support may provide store credit as per internal replacement matrix.

Safety and Usage Conditions:
Only certified charging adapters and cables should be used with DROMA devices.
Overheating due to incompatible third-party accessories is outside warranty scope.
Damage from water exposure, burnt pins, or physical cuts in cable insulation is not covered.

Post-Replacement Terms:
Replacement accessories carry either the remaining original warranty or 90 days, whichever is longer.
Repeated failure due to device-side power issues may require device diagnostics before another accessory claim is approved.
