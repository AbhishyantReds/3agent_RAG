DROMA Electronics Laptop Warranty Policy:
All laptops include a 1-year manufacturer warranty from date of purchase.
Battery health degradation is treated as normal wear and is not covered after 6 months.
Screen damage and liquid damage are not covered under standard warranty.
Customers must provide invoice and serial number for service requests.
Average repair turnaround time is 5 to 7 business days.

Extended Laptop Service Details:
Warranty covers manufacturing defects in motherboard, SSD/HDD, RAM, keyboard controller, and factory-fitted Wi-Fi modules.
Warranty does not cover cosmetic wear, dents, scratches, cracked hinges due to impact, or damage caused by pests.
Data recovery is not included in warranty service and may be billed separately if requested.
OS reinstallation due to malware or user-side software issues is treated as paid service unless device is under a DROMA Care plan.

Dead on Arrival (DOA) Policy:
If a laptop fails within 7 calendar days of delivery and passes diagnostics for manufacturing fault, it is eligible for replacement review.
Replacement review requires unboxing video or delivery condition proof, original packaging, and all bundled accessories.
Final replacement approval is issued after authorized service center inspection.

Repair and Escalation Timelines:
Minor issues such as keyboard replacement or fan cleaning are usually completed in 3 to 5 business days.
Major board-level repair may take 7 to 12 business days depending on part availability.
If a required part is out of stock for more than 14 days, customers may request escalation for alternative resolution.

Claim Process:
Customers can register claims via DROMA chat, helpline, or nearest authorized center.
Required documents: proof of purchase, device serial number, and customer contact details.
Out-of-warranty diagnostic fee is communicated before repair approval.

Battery and Adapter Notes:
Battery swelling, usage pattern impact, and performance loss over time are treated under battery-specific policy.
Original DROMA adapter usage is recommended; third-party charger damage is not covered.

Warranty Validity Conditions:
Warranty is valid only when product serial number is intact and readable.
Unauthorized opening, soldering, or third-party board modification voids manufacturer warranty.
Service quality and part authenticity are guaranteed only at authorized DROMA centers.
